14. Ship Rules

  • 14.1 Ships are to be no larger than 80x50 tiles.
  • 14.2 Do not abuse the ship save mechanic to avoid in-character consequences.
  • 14.3 Do not exploit the stash to duplicate items. This especially goes for contraband.
  • 14.4 Do not use inventories such as lockers to circumvent stash restrictions.
  • 14.5 Ship saving backups are only to be used in the event of ship destruction or loss due to a server crash or other unforeseen issue. Loading backups otherwise is only to be done with admin permission.
  • 14.6 Do not under any circumstances modify a ship save file without admin permission.
  • 14.7 Do not go AFK for extended periods on your ship (like logging off to sleep). The ship saving is provided to allow people to save progress without the need to do this. UNATTENDED SHIPS WILL BE REMOVED.
  • 14.8 DO NOT ADD MORE THAN ONE BLUESPACE STASH PER SHIP.
  • 14.9 Members of command are not to leave the station and use their ships.
